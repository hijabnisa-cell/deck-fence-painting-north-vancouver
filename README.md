## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why should decks and fences be painted or coated?
Protective coatings help shield wood from weather exposure, moisture, and ultraviolet rays while improving appearance.

### Is preparation important before painting exterior wood?
Cleaning, sanding, and repairing damaged areas help coatings bond more effectively and last longer.

### Can old paint be applied over without preparation?
Loose or peeling paint should generally be removed to improve adhesion and reduce premature failure.

### How often should outdoor wood surfaces be maintained?
Maintenance schedules vary depending on climate, sun exposure, and the condition of previous coatings.

### Does weather affect painting projects?
Temperature, humidity, and precipitation can influence drying and curing conditions.

### Are decks and fences treated differently?
Because they experience different wear patterns and exposure, preparation and coating choices may differ.

### What happens if moisture is trapped under paint?
Excess moisture can contribute to peeling, blistering, or reduced coating performance.

### Can damaged boards be painted?
Structural or severely deteriorated boards are often repaired or replaced before painting begins.
